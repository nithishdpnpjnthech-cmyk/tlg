IT Hardware Leasing Services

TLG Infotech offers flexible rental solutions for a wide range of IT and security products to support short‑term and long‑term business requirements. Our portfolio includes network switches, firewalls, servers, computer systems, NVRs, DVRs and other infrastructure equipment — all available with full technical support and fast deployment.

We provide comprehensive IT equipment leasing including:
  • Laptops, desktops & workstations
  • Servers & storage systems
  • Networking equipment (switches, routers, firewalls)
  • Wi‑Fi infrastructure (controllers & access points)
  • AV equipment for meetings & events
  • Printers & peripheral devices
Also available
  • Temporary project or event hardware setups
  • Bulk deployment for corporate environments
  • Short‑term & long‑term leasing options
  • Pre‑configured systems ready for deployment
  • Upgrade & replacement options
  • On‑site installation & setup support
  • Maintenance & technical assistance
