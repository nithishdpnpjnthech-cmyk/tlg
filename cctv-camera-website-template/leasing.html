Configuration & Rental Services

Configuration & Rental Services provide businesses with professionally prepared IT and AV equipment that is configured, tested, and deployed based on operational requirements. These services help organizations quickly scale infrastructure, support short-term projects, events, or temporary expansions without heavy capital investment.

By combining expert configuration with flexible rental options, businesses gain ready-to-use systems that ensure smooth performance, faster deployment, and operational efficiency.

What Comes Under Configuration & Rental Services

We provide end-to-end equipment preparation and rental support including:

  • Device configuration & pre-deployment setup
  • Network equipment configuration
  • Software installation & system optimization
  • AV equipment configuration & testing
  • Temporary IT infrastructure rental
  • Event & project-based equipment rental
  • Laptops, desktops & workstation rentals
  • Networking & connectivity equipment rental
  • Surveillance & access equipment rental
  • Meeting room & AV setup rental
  • Onsite deployment & commissioning
  • Equipment testing & quality assurance
  • Backup & standby device provisioning
  • Short-term & long-term rental options
  • Maintenance & technical support during rental
