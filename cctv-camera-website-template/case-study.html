Enterprise Campus CCTV & Network Upgrade
Challenge

The existing analog system caused poor image quality and gaps in coverage across multiple buildings. Network segmentation and intermittent connectivity led to incomplete recordings and slow response.

Solution

We migrated to an IP-based solution with PoE cameras, centralized NVR/recording, VLAN segmentation, and redundant switching. Edge analytics was enabled for motion detection and license-plate capture where required.

Results
  • Improved image quality and coverage with HD IP cameras.
  • Centralized monitoring reduced response time by 65%.
  • 99.9% system availability thanks to redundant networking.

Client quote: "TLG delivered a reliable solution with measurable security improvements."

CCTV Rollout for Retail Chain
Challenge
