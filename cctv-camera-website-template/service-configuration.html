Technology Products & Components

Unmanaged Network Switches

Unmanaged switches provide simple, plug-and-play connectivity without configuration. They are ideal for small offices and basic network expansion where ease of use and cost-effectiveness are priorities.

Smart / Web-Managed Switches

Smart switches offer basic management features such as VLANs, traffic prioritization, and monitoring through a web interface. These switches are suitable for growing businesses that need more control than unmanaged switches without complex configurations.

Managed Network Switches

Managed switches provide full network control, monitoring, and security. They support advanced features such as VLANs, QoS, network segmentation, and remote management, making them ideal for enterprise and mission-critical networks.

Layer 2 (L2) Switches

Layer 2 switches operate at the data link layer and efficiently manage data traffic within a local network. They are commonly used for access-level switching in offices, campuses, and enterprise environments.

Layer 3 (L3) Switches

Layer 3 switches combine switching and routing capabilities, enabling high-speed data routing between networks. These switches are ideal for core and distribution layers in large enterprise and data center networks.

PoE (Power over Ethernet) Switches

PoE switches deliver power and data over a single Ethernet cable. They are commonly used to support IP cameras, wireless access points, VoIP phones, and access control systems, reducing cabling complexity.

Gigabit Ethernet Switches

Gigabit switches support high-speed data transfer up to 1 Gbps, ensuring fast and reliable network performance for modern applications and enterprise workloads.

10G / High Speed Switches

High-speed switches support 10G and higher bandwidths for data centers, backbone networks, and high performance environments requiring low latency and high throughput.

Core Switches

Core switches form the backbone of large networks, handling high volumes of traffic with maximum reliability and redundancy. They are designed for high availability and scalability.

Access Switches

Access switches connect end-user devices such as computers, printers, and IP phones to the network. They provide secure and efficient connectivity at the edge of the network.

Industrial Network Switches

Industrial switches are designed to operate in harsh environments with extended temperature ranges and rugged enclosures. They are suitable for factories, warehouses, and outdoor installations.

Data Center Switches

Data center switches support high-density ports, ultra-low latency, and high bandwidth to meet the demands of modern data center environments and cloud infrastructure.

Integrated Switching Solutions

TLG Infotech delivers switching products as part of fully integrated IT infrastructure solutions. Our approach focuses on compatibility, scalability, security, and long-term performance to support evolving business needs.

Next Generation Firewalls (NGFW)

Next generation firewalls provide advanced threat protection with deep packet inspection, application control, intrusion prevention, and malware defense. They secure enterprise networks against evolving cyber threats.

Unified Threat Management (UTM) Firewalls

UTM firewalls combine multiple security functions such as firewall, antivirus, intrusion prevention, and web filtering into a single platform. They are ideal for small and medium businesses seeking comprehensive security with simplified management.

Hardware Firewalls

Hardware firewalls offer dedicated, high performance network protection for on-premise environments. They ensure reliable security and consistent performance for enterprise and data center networks.

Virtual / Cloud Firewalls

Virtual firewalls provide flexible and scalable security for cloud and virtualized environments. They support secure remote access, cloud workloads, and hybrid network architectures.

VPN Firewalls

VPN enabled firewalls provide secure remote connectivity through encrypted tunnels, enabling safe access to enterprise networks for remote users and branch offices.

IP Cameras

IP cameras deliver high resolution digital video and support remote monitoring over networks. They enable intelligent surveillance, analytics, and centralized video management.

Analog HD Cameras

Analog HD cameras provide cost effective surveillance with reliable performance and improved image quality over traditional analog systems.

PTZ Cameras

PTZ (Pan Tilt Zoom) cameras offer flexible movement and optical zoom for wide area monitoring. They are ideal for large spaces such as campuses, warehouses, and parking areas.

Dome Cameras

Dome cameras feature a compact and discreet design, making them suitable for indoor and outdoor surveillance with wide viewing angles.

Bullet Cameras

Bullet cameras are designed for long range outdoor surveillance. Their focused viewing angle makes them ideal for perimeter security and entry-exit monitoring.

Thermal Cameras

Thermal cameras detect heat signatures and are effective in low-light or no-visibility conditions. They are commonly used for critical infrastructure and high-security environments.

Professional Displays

Professional displays include LED, LCD, and interactive screens designed for meeting rooms, control rooms, and digital signage applications.

Projectors

High performance projectors provide large format visual display solutions for auditoriums, training rooms, and conference spaces.

Audio Systems

Professional audio systems include microphones, speakers, amplifiers, and DSPs that deliver clear and balanced sound for meetings, events, and presentations.

Video Conferencing Systems

Video conferencing products enable real time collaboration through high definition video, audio, and content sharing across locations.
